About me

My name is Li Shuo and code name is Aleda. I am Aleda at Github and @AledaLee at Sina Weibo. You can also reach me via email.

I am a intern on Baidu which is a search engine company. I play a broad role there - which includes research, product design, engineering and deployment. I also lend a hand in user support.

I try to contribute to society by striving to create great software products that make people's lives easier. I believe software is the most effective way to touch others' lives in our day and time.

I mostly work in C, C++ and Linux, I also dabble in Python, Vim-L and Shell. I do not pigeonhole myself to specific languages or frameworks. A good developer is receptive and has the ability to learn new technologies. I also often contribute to open source projects and beta test startup products.

Besides programming, I exercise and read books regularly. To be a stronger and better version of myself!
